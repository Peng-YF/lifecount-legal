# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

LifeCount respects your privacy. This app is designed to function without collecting or transmitting your personal data.

## 1. Data Collection

LifeCount does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal data to any external servers.

All information you enter in the app (such as date of birth, region, or preferences) is:

* Stored locally on your device
* Used only to calculate time-related visualizations
* Never shared with the developer or third parties

## 2. Analytics & Tracking

LifeCount:

* Does **not** use third-party analytics
* Does **not** track users across apps or websites
* Does **not** use advertising identifiers (IDFA)

## 3. In-App Purchases

LifeCount offers optional in-app purchases (subscription and lifetime unlock). Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. The app does not receive or store payment information.

## 4. Data Sharing

LifeCount does **not** share data with any third parties.

## 5. Data Deletion

Because LifeCount does not collect or store user data externally, uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

LifeCount is not intended for children under the age of 13. No data is knowingly collected from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

If the app’s functionality changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated accordingly.
